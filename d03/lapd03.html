The Orchid Flower, Its Meaning And Symbolism

Orchids are the lagest family of blooming flower with over 25000 species and over 100000 varieties. They are often grown as houseplant, or added to floral display. But not all orchid are tropical beauties. Wild orchid grow worldwile and can be found on every continent expect Antarctica. These flowers have earned the reputation as difficult to grow, probatly due to their need for filtered light and high relative humnidity. Many varieties, such as the moth orchid (Phalaenopis) are surprisingly easy to grow as a houseplant.

What Dose The Orchid Flower Mean

The orchid has been held in high regard since ancient times. It symbolizes

  • Love
  • Beauty
  • Fertility
  • Refinement
  • Thoughtfulness
  • Charm
